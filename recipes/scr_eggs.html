Scrambled Eggs

Description

The secret to making scrambled eggs is all about patience...and the proper pan. Here's a quick step-by-step guide to show you how to make scrambled eggs, plus we'll answer your most frequently asked questions.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 eggs
  • 4-6 tablespoons of milk
  • salt and pepper
  • Optional: chopped fresh parsley or other herbs

Steps

  1. Crack eggs on a flat surface
  2. Add a liquid such as milk, cream, lemon juice or even a little water
  3. Whisk thoroughly
  4. Heat the skillet
  5. Add the eggs and stir
  6. Add more flavor
  7. Finish the eggs
